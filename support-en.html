Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I share with family?
    Go to the My tab and use the family invite code to share access.
  • I'm not receiving push notifications.
    Go to iPhone Settings → Poopie Mate → Notifications and make sure notifications are enabled.
  • Walk safety isn't showing weather data.
    Please check that location access is enabled in your device settings.
  • How do I delete my account or data?
    Use Settings → Account Settings → Delete Account in the app, or send us an email and we'll handle it promptly.
