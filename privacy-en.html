Poopie Mate (응가메이트)

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

Pixelberry ("we," "our," or "the Company") is committed to protecting your personal information in connection with the operation of the Poopie Mate service ("Service"). We comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

The following information may be collected when you use the Service:

  • Social login information — Email address, user ID, display name, and profile photo from your Google, Apple, or Kakao account (limited to what is provided at the time of login)
  • Pet information — Your dog's name, photo, size, age, body condition, coat type, and health information
  • Potty records — Timestamps and status of poop and pee logs, and walk records
  • Location information — Current location coordinates for walk weather checks and weather-based alerts (only if you grant permission)
  • Device or app identifiers — Device or app identifiers such as Expo Push Tokens for push notifications
  • Service settings — Language, notification settings, preferred walk windows, potty goals, and other app settings

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide potty and walk tracking and family-sharing features
  • To retrieve weather data for walk safety assessments
  • To send personalized push notifications for potty timing and walk schedules
  • To create accounts, authenticate users, manage family invitations, and provide account management
  • To improve service quality, respond to errors, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations

3. Retention Period

We retain personal information until you delete your account or submit a deletion request. When account deletion is completed, we delete the account and related data from our service database.

However, information required by applicable law, minimal information needed for dispute handling, and customer support records may be retained for the necessary period and then deleted. Information that remains temporarily in backups or system logs is access-restricted and deleted or anonymized within 30 days.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When you have given prior consent
  • When required by law or requested by a law enforcement authority

5. Service Providers and External Services

We use the following service providers or external services to operate the Service:

  • Supabase Inc. — Database and authentication services
  • Expo (Expo Go / EAS) — App builds and push notifications
  • Google LLC — Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
  • Google LLC, Apple Inc., Kakao Corp. — Social login authentication
  • Open-Meteo — Weather forecast lookup based on location coordinates

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information
  • Request restriction of processing of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and related data
  • Request deletion or correction of some data without deleting your account

7. Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your Poopie Mate account and related data using the following methods:

  • In-app deletion — Open the app → My → Account Settings → Delete Account → Confirm
  • Email request — If you cannot use the app, you can request account deletion by emailing jin@pixelberry.io.

When you delete your account, we delete service data including your service profile connected to your social login account, pet information and photos, poop, pee, and walk records, family connections and invitations, notification settings, location coordinates, walk safety profile, push notification tokens, and notification history.

Information required by law, minimal information needed for dispute handling, and customer support records may be retained for the necessary period. Information that remains in backups or system logs is deleted or anonymized within 30 days.

If you want to delete only some data without deleting your account, you can delete individual poop, pee, and walk records in the Records screen, or contact jin@pixelberry.io for additional deletion or correction requests.

8. Location Information

Location information is used to check walk weather, calculate walk safety, and provide weather-based alerts. If you grant location permission, your current location coordinates may be sent to a weather lookup API and may be stored in our service database to provide server-based alerts. You can still use core features such as potty logging and family sharing without granting location permission.

For any questions, complaints, or requests regarding your personal information, please contact us at the email above and we will respond promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will notify you via an in-app notice or on this page before the changes take effect.
