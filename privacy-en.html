Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

Pixelberry ("we," "our," or "the Company") is committed to protecting your personal information in connection with the operation of the Poopie Mate service ("Service"). We comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

The following information may be collected when you use the Service:

  • Social login information — Email address, display name, and profile photo from your Google or Apple account (limited to what is provided at the time of login)
  • Pet information — Your dog's name, photo, size, age, body condition, coat type, and health information
  • Potty records — Timestamps and status of poop and pee logs, and walk records
  • Location information — Your current location for weather lookups (only if you grant permission)
  • Device information — Push notification token

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide potty and walk tracking and family-sharing features
  • To retrieve weather data for walk safety assessments
  • To send personalized push notifications for potty timing and walk schedules
  • To improve service quality and respond to errors

3. Retention Period

We retain your information until you delete your account or submit a deletion request. Where applicable law requires retention for a specific period, we will retain and then securely delete the data accordingly.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When you have given prior consent
  • When required by law or requested by a law enforcement authority

5. Service Providers

We use the following third-party service providers to operate the Service:

  • Supabase Inc. — Database and authentication services
  • Expo (Expo Go / EAS) — App builds and push notifications
  • Google LLC — Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information
  • Request restriction of processing of your personal information
  • To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at jin@pixelberry.io. We will process your request within 3 business days.

7. Location Information

Location data is used solely to check weather conditions for walk safety and is never stored on our servers. Core features (potty logging, family sharing) are fully available without granting location permission.
